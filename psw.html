Leen On Me is not a psw client matching database, we are a full service company that hires its employees and provides our clients with affordable customized home care. We select the most compassionate psw and are committed to preserving our clients' dignity by attending to their preferences and fostering independence while promoting safety. We provide care at all levels, including caregiver releife and specialty care for those with dementia, heart conditions, or arthritis.
Personal Support Worker
From brief visits to live-in home care, we have you covered. We screen and train all employees to ensure they will uphold our high standards, we will work to provide you with the best fit possible in all situations. This approach aims to connect clients with our psw on a more personal level. The reward in this is that it transforms the client relationship into one based on friendship and trust.
Sometimes your loved one may need more specialized care than basic personal care in order to live safely and independently at home. This may include patients with diseases such as Alzheimer's or people who have recently undergone a surgery. Leen On Me offers personalized and dependable home care in situations like this. Our expert paw will provide you or your loved ones the care they need.
