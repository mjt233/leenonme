Home Care Services for Children, Teenagers, Adults and Seniors

Leen On Me is committed to providing affordable, reliable, compassionate, and personalized care to enrich the quality of one's life. Our priority is to Increase Independence and Decrease Isolation through one-on-one support. We throughly screen and match our fully certified professional caregivers to the clients personal needs. Our staff of professionals are friendly, caring, experienced and they truly enjoy what they do.
