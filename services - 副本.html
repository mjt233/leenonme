Leen On Me Home Care Services carefully screens and matches our clients with his\her caregiver. Our staff of professionals are friendly, caring, experienced, honest health care providers with unique personalities that are sure to gain the confidence of each client. Our caregivers are dedicated to your social and emotional well-being. Fully Customizable Services

Personal Caregiver

From head to toe, your loved one wants to feel and look their best. Our personal care and hygiene services can assist with a wide range of care and grooming depending on your loved one's needs. Leen On Me keeps each client clean, comfortable, and looking their best at all times. Our staff can assist with the following for everyday activities;

* Personal hygiene and grooming

* Incontinence Care

* Assistance with mobility

* Help with errands

* Companionship

* Meal Preparation

Personal Support Worker

Services include a wide variety of supportive care provided in the home or residence.

* Assisting or providing total personal care such as toileting, bathing

* Mobility Support

* Assisting with Eating/Feeding (G-tube)

* Escorting to Appointments

* Transition To Home

* Monitor changes in a patient’s condition

* Assisting with ambulation, positioning and transferring using mechanical lifts

Companionship

Our Caregivers are available for individualized activities such as visiting, playing cards, reading a book, or sharing a hobby. We can also help care for a pet or take walks in the neighborhood.

Caregiver Relief

We are dedicated to providing relief for family caregivers, from a few hours a day to around the clock care. Our caregivers are trained to tend to the needs of your loved ones while you attend to other responsibilities and obligations, or just put your feet up and relax for a while to rejuvenate.

Meal Preparation

Learn how to prepare simple yet delicious meals in the comfort of your home. We will help you or your loved one learn there way around the kitchen so they can become more independent and make great meals for the family.

Light housekeeping

Leen On Me cleaning services will take care of all you light housekeeping and cleaning duties. No need to worry about keeping your loved one's home clean.

* Parent/Guardian Date Nights

* Caregiver Relief

* Over night Trips

Transportation

We can take you or your loved one to and from appointments, to visit family and friends, team games. We offer grocery delivery serivce right to your front door.
