Who are we?

We are a fully licensed, full-service home care services Agency located in Kingston Ontario.

Who/ What are we looking for?

Nice, reliable, experienced, caregivers that can do live-out/ live-in and overnight shifts to help us with our mission statement to help Increase Independence and Decrease Isolation and to be safe, healthy and happy at home.

It takes a special type of person to be a non-medical home care provider. With this profession comes the great privilege and responsibility of sharing in people’s life experiences, during both happy and difficult times. Often the caregiver assumes several roles, acting as the care provider, friend, confidant, advocate and more. It takes great interpersonal and professional skills to promote the client’s choice, health, independence and general well being.

With these challenges also comes the wonderful opportunity for personal and professional growth as a caregiver. In an industry that is constantly changing, there are various areas of opportunity that allow a senior caregiver and a specialized disability caregiver to make a real difference in someone’s life.

Leen On Me recognizes that caregivers play a crucial role in the delivery of quality home care services. We also know that in order to make a positive impact, caregivers need sustained support in performing their job. That’s why Leen On Me takes an individualized approach to referrals and case coordination in order to ensure that our caregivers’ needs are always met.
